  var age: Int!

}

The assignment:

Define a Car class. A car will at least have brand name, maximum speed and coordinate (x, y). You will need to create three instances of Car with different brand names and maximum speed, but the same coordinate (0,0). Then enable all the cars to move by a certain distance vector (x,y). For example, a car at (3,4) will move to (2,5) by (-1,1). Please make sure cars are able to move multiple times.
